Sarah Battersby

As a researcher, my areas is primarily is Geographic Information Science (GIScience), with specialization in cognitive cartography and visualization. My research emphasizes theories and tools for visualizing information, cognitive research in the role of visualization in problem solving, and map projections. I also have strong interest in development of critical spatial thinking abilities through formal and informal geospatial education.
